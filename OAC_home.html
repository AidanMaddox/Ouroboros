OUR STORY

From as early as the Thirteenth Century B.C.E., the symbol of a serpent devouring its own tail has served as an emblem of wholeness, rebirth, and infinity. Known as “Ouroboros”, it guides the way that we craft our work, with proceeds donated to protect the very planet from which our products were born. Ultimately, we aim to make preservation second nature, integrating social awareness into every product that we sell and inspiring donations from sources that are otherwise untapped.

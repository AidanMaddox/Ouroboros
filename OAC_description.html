About Our Product

Each of our bracelets is meticulously threaded, tied, and then glued to ensure a quality fit and reliable hold. Once finished, the bracelet is placed into an iconic Ouroboros burlap pouch, with a card about us, a card telling you more about your bracelet, and finally a sticker, to deck out anything you desire. All told, here at Ouroboros Apparel Co., we are committed to crafting the best products and experiences for every single client.



Steps of Production

1. Choose which beads to use for your bracelet


2. Cut a length of string a couple inches larger than your wrist


3. Carefully thread the beads onto the string. If you have an accent bead, have an equal amount of main beads on each side


4. Once threaded, tie the ends of the bracelets in a box knot as close to the beads as possible


5. Use superglue to secure the knot. We modeled and 3D printed a tool that held the knot separate from the beads


6. Once dry, remove the bracelet and cut the excess string with an exacto knife

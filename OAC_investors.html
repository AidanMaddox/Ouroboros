THE PROBLEM


Destruction of forests, illegal hunting, and global warming put the animals we love at great risk.

THE SOLUTION?


Ouroboros Apparel Co.

Ouroboros is making a difference by both donating directly to the conservation of endangered animal species, as well as providing an opportunity that sparks the discussion of preservation. 100% of profit from our bracelets go directly to the Wildlife Conservation Network, an organization with a perfect score from Charity Navigator, who are dedicated to the preservation of several endangered species. The Wildlife Conservation Network puts 100% of its recieved donations towards smaller, and more speciialized groups with the same structure, ensuring that every dollar goes towards helping to restore each species.
